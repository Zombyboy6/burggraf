# Burggraf

Build, upgrade and defend your castle against **waves** of invaders!

This is a tower defense game created for the [Game Off 2025](https://itch.io/jam/game-off-2025) Game Jam.

You can play a nightly release [here](https://zombyboy6.github.io/burggraf/). But be warned it may not work as intended or at all.

# Gameplay

- **Gather Resources**: Collect Wood and Stone from the surrounding area to construct and expand your castle defenses
- **Build Your Economy**: Harvest Food and construct houses to recruit soldiers and workers, generating a steady income of gold
- **Unlock & Upgrade**: Spend gold to research new buildings, defensive structures, and powerful upgrades to strengthen your castle against increasingly challenging waves of enemies
